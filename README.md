# 🍹 Gasoline in my Piña Colada

**A game made by a team of 5** for the **Comfy Jam: Summer** using **Unreal Engine 5** and **C++** built in **14 days**.

## Play It

[Try it on itch.io !](https://jungor.itch.io/ginpc)

---

## Introduction

**Game Jam theme :** *"Instructions are a lie"*

You play as a **student** who got a summer job at a beach bar, whipping up cocktails for thirsty customers under the watchful eye of a suspicious manager.

There's also a bottle of gasoline on your counter for some reason.

### Description

Drag ingredients into the blender or shaker, mix your cocktails, and serve them to customers before their patience runs dry. Follow the recipe and the manager's instructions.

Or don't.

There are **3 different endings** depending on the choices make. The game doesn't tell which path leads where.
